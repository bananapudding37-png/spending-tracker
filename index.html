Where is my data stored?
Everything is saved in your browser's localStorage — on this device, in this browser only. It stays there until you clear your browser data or cache. To keep your data safe long-term, host this file on GitHub Pages (data is per-browser) and avoid clearing site data. No server, no account needed.
